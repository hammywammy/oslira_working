Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription + credits model work? +
Each monthly subscription includes a set number of credits that refresh every month. You can purchase additional credits at your plan's discounted rate anytime. Features like API access, support, and processing speeds remain active as long as your subscription is current.
What happens to unused credits at the end of the month? +
Unused monthly credits roll over for up to 3 months, giving you flexibility for varying usage patterns. After 3 months, unused credits expire to keep the system fair and sustainable.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription? +
Yes, you can change your subscription tier anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle to ensure you get full value from your current plan.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? +
You'll retain access to all features until the end of your current billing period. Any remaining credits will be available for 90 days after cancellation, giving you time to use them or resubscribe.
What's included in a "deep analysis"? +
A deep analysis includes comprehensive AI-powered insights, data processing, visualization generation, and detailed reporting. Each analysis provides actionable intelligence from your data.
Is there a free trial available? +
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of any plan with full access to features and included credits. No credit card required to start your trial.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and enterprise invoicing for larger packages. All transactions are secured with enterprise-grade encryption.
