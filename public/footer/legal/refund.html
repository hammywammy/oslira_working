Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 12, 2025
Last Updated: January 12, 2025

💡 Understanding Our Refund Policy

Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent while protecting the integrity of our credit-based service model. Please read this policy carefully before making any purchases.

1. General Refund Policy

1.1 Subscription Refunds

Monthly subscriptions may be eligible for pro-rated refunds under specific circumstances:

Scenario Refund Eligibility Processing Time
Billing error or duplicate charge Full refund available 3-5 business days
Service unavailable for 24+ hours Pro-rated credit or refund 5-7 business days
Cancellation within 48 hours of purchase Full refund (unused credits only) 3-5 business days
Standard cancellation No refund, service continues until period end N/A

1.2 Additional Credit Purchases

Additional credits purchased beyond your subscription allowance are generally non-refundable due to their digital nature and instant availability. However, exceptions may apply in cases of:

  • Billing errors or unauthorized charges
  • Technical failures preventing credit usage
  • Documented service quality issues

2. Refund Eligibility Criteria

✅ Eligible for Refund Consideration

The following situations may qualify for refunds on a case-by-case basis:

2.1 Technical Issues

  • Platform downtime exceeding 24 hours
  • Critical bugs preventing service usage
  • Data loss due to system failures
  • AI analysis failures due to platform errors

2.2 Billing Errors

  • Duplicate charges or processing errors
  • Unauthorized charges or account access
  • Incorrect subscription tier billing
  • Failed payment reversals

2.3 Service Quality Issues

  • Demonstrable AI analysis quality problems
  • Persistent platform performance issues
  • Features not working as advertised

3. Non-Refundable Items

❌ Not Eligible for Refunds

The following purchases are final and non-refundable under normal circumstances:

3.1 Used Credits

Credits that have been consumed for lead analysis cannot be refunded, as they represent completed AI processing and delivered insights.

3.2 Subscription Services After 48 Hours

Subscription charges older than 48 hours are generally non-refundable, though service will continue until the end of the paid period.

3.3 Promotional Credits

Free credits, bonus credits, or promotional offerings cannot be refunded or converted to cash value.

3.4 Third-Party Service Failures

Refunds are not available for issues caused by third-party platform changes (e.g., Instagram policy updates) that may affect our service capabilities.

4. Refund Request Process

4.1 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at billing@oslira.com
  2. Include your account email and transaction details
  3. Provide a detailed explanation of the issue
  4. Submit supporting documentation if applicable

4.2 Review Process

Our team will review refund requests within 3-5 business days. We may request additional information or attempt to resolve the issue through alternative means before processing a refund.

4.3 Refund Methods

Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method within 5-10 business days. In some cases, we may offer account credits as an alternative solution.

5. Chargeback and Dispute Policy

⚠️ Chargeback Warning

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting our support team may result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of all credits and data.

5.1 Before Filing a Chargeback

Please contact our support team first. Most billing issues can be resolved quickly without involving your bank or credit card company.

5.2 Consequences of Unauthorized Chargebacks

  • Immediate suspension of platform access
  • Forfeiture of all unused credits
  • Loss of account data and analysis history
  • Potential permanent account termination

6. European Union Consumer Rights

🇪🇺 EU Consumer Protection

EU consumers have additional rights under European consumer protection laws, including a 14-day cooling-off period for certain digital services.

For EU customers, the right of withdrawal may apply to subscription services within 14 days of purchase, provided the service has not been fully performed with your consent.

7. Cancellation vs. Refunds

7.1 How Cancellation Works

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time
  • Service continues until the end of your current billing period
  • No additional charges will occur after cancellation
  • Unused credits remain available until account closure

7.2 When Refunds Apply to Cancellations

Refunds are only provided for cancellations in exceptional circumstances as outlined in this policy.

8. Contact Information

Need Help with Billing or Refunds?

Billing Support: billing@oslira.com
General Support: support@oslira.com
Response Time: Within 24 hours on business days

We're committed to resolving billing issues fairly and quickly. Please contact us before taking any other action.
